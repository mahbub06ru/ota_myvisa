Course Curriculum

Module 1: IELTS Listening (Band 0-9)

  • Understanding IELTS Listening test format and question types
  • Strategies for multiple choice, matching, map/plan/diagram labeling
  • Note completion, form completion, and sentence completion techniques
  • Time management and concentration skills
  • Common pitfalls and how to avoid them
  • Practice tests with detailed feedback

Module 2: IELTS Reading (Band 0-9)

  • Academic vs General Training Reading differences
  • Skimming and scanning techniques
  • True/False/Not Given and Yes/No/Not Given strategies
  • Matching headings, information, and features
  • Summary completion and short answer questions
  • Vocabulary building and paraphrasing skills

Module 3: IELTS Writing (Band 0-9)

  • Task 1: Academic (Graphs, Charts, Diagrams) and General (Letter Writing)
  • Task 2: Essay Writing (Opinion, Discussion, Problem-Solution, Advantages/Disadvantages)
  • Essay structure and paragraph development
  • Coherence and cohesion techniques
  • Lexical resource and grammatical range
  • Time management and word count strategies

Module 4: IELTS Speaking (Band 0-9)

  • Part 1: Introduction and Interview (Personal questions)
  • Part 2: Long Turn (Cue card preparation and delivery)
  • Part 3: Two-way Discussion (Abstract topics)
  • Pronunciation, fluency, and coherence
  • Vocabulary and grammatical accuracy
  • Mock speaking tests with personalized feedback

Module 5: Test Strategies & Practice

  • Full-length mock tests under exam conditions
  • Score prediction and band improvement strategies
  • Time management across all modules
  • Common mistakes and how to avoid them
  • Test day preparation and tips
  • Personalized feedback and improvement plans

Note: The curriculum includes comprehensive practice materials, official IELTS practice tests, sample answers, vocabulary lists, and access to our online resource library. All four modules are covered in depth with extensive practice opportunities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the difference between IELTS Academic and General Training?

A: IELTS Academic is for those applying for higher education or professional registration, while IELTS General Training is for migration purposes and work experience. Our course covers both formats, and we'll help you determine which one you need based on your goals.

Q: How long does it take to prepare for IELTS?

A: Preparation time varies based on your current English level and target band score. Our 30-class course (60 hours) provides comprehensive coverage, but we recommend 2-3 months of consistent practice. Students typically see improvement within 4-6 weeks of focused preparation.

Q: Will I receive study materials and practice tests?

A: Yes! All students receive comprehensive study materials, official IELTS practice tests, sample answers, vocabulary lists, and access to our online resource library. We also provide mock tests under exam conditions to help you prepare.

Q: What is the payment structure and are there any discounts?

A: The course fee is BDT 15,000. You can pay in two installments (BDT 7,500 each) or get a discount of BDT 3,000 by paying the full amount (BDT 12,000) at the time of admission. The first installment is due at enrollment, and the second installment is due after the first month.

Q: What happens if I miss a class?

A: We understand that students may occasionally miss classes. We provide recorded sessions (for online classes) and supplementary materials. You can also schedule a one-on-one session with the instructor to catch up on missed content. However, regular attendance is highly recommended for the best learning experience.

Q: Will I get personalized feedback on my writing and speaking?

A: Absolutely! Our course includes personalized feedback sessions for both Writing and Speaking modules. You'll receive detailed comments on your essays, speaking practice, and specific areas for improvement. We also conduct mock speaking tests to simulate the actual exam experience.

Q: What band score can I expect after completing this course?

A: Your final band score depends on your starting level, dedication, and practice. Our course is designed to help you achieve your target score. Students typically improve by 0.5-1.5 bands after completing the course. We provide initial assessment tests to help set realistic targets and track your progress.
